FAQ

Is an original piece of your work?

Indeed, every print I offer is an original work. They are exclusively photographs I have captured personally.


Do you provide the option for digital downloads so I can print the image on my own?

Certainly, all prints featured on my website have an option for digital download. This allows you the flexibility to print the image yourself. The provided file is a high-resolution jpeg, enabling you to produce prints in all the sizes listed on my website.


How would you describe the quality of your prints?

I ensure all my prints maintain a consistent, high-quality standard. They are produced on museum-grade, Fine Art 310GSM, archival cotton rag paper. With a matte finish and a delightful texture, these 310GSM prints are thick and the quality is instantly apparent.


What are the exact dimensions of your prints?

Here are the precise dimensions of the prints in mm: 400mm x 600mm, 600mm x 900mm, and 800mm x 1200mm.


Do your prints come with white borders?

Indeed, all of my prints feature a white border surrounding the image. The size of this border depends on the print size and whether or not the print is framed. Unframed prints generally have a 15mm white border.


How do you package your prints?

Prints are either packaged in a tube or flat packed depending on the size of the print. Only dense protective tubes or strong flat pack materials are used to ensure the safety and security whilst shipping.


Do you offer prints with frames?

Yes, framed prints are available. The options include: 400mm x 600mm, 600mm x 900mm, and 800mm x 1200mm, all with varying frame profiles. Prints are dry mounted to an acid-free 5mm foam board using an archival adhesive film. Each print is housed in a handcrafted shadow box frame made from FSC certified Victorian Ash and fronted with durable 3mm museum-grade plexiglass. The frame is available in a natural, black, or white finish.


How are your framed prints packaged?

Framed prints are securely packed using Polycell EcoPure biodegradable bubble wrap and cartons made of 77% recycled content and 33% FSC certified kraft fibres.


What are the shipping charges?

For Australia, both unframed and framed prints are shipped for free. International customers purchasing unframed prints are charged a standard flat fee of $50. For international shipping of framed prints, please reach out for a custom quote specific to your location.


If I purchase a single print or two, how can I have it framed elsewhere?

Having your prints framed is a simple process. You can locate a framing service in your locality with a quick Google search. Consider their reviews and choose one that suits your needs. They will assist you in selecting your frame and border options and will typically handle the entire process, including delivery and hanging, if required.

Yes. Feel free to send me a message


I represent a brand and would like to book you for a private shoot.

Of course, simply send me a message and we can initiate the process.


Can I schedule a private shoot?

Yes, you certainly can! Just send me a message and we can plan an exceptional photo shoot just for you!
