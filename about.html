BEHIND THE LENS

Hello, I'm Josy, a passionate photographer originally from Brazil and currently based in the beautiful coastal town of Noosa, QLD, Australia. I have a deep appreciation for the ocean, surfing, beaches, skateboarding and the vibrant coastal lifestyle that surrounds me. Photography has always been my way of capturing the world's breathtaking moments. I love immersing myself in different cultures and seeking out unique experiences to capture through my lens. Whether it's the raw power of crashing waves, a radical manouver, the serene beauty of golden sunsets, or the authentic expressions of people, I strive to encapsulate the essence of life in my photographs. Living in Noosa has been a dream come true, as it offers an incredible blend of nature and culture. The stunning beaches, the relaxed lifestyle, and the vibrant surf community all provide endless inspiration for my work. I believe that photography has the power to evoke emotions, tell stories, and inspire others. It's a way for me to share my passion and perspective with the world. Through my images, I aim to transport viewers to the very moments I capture and evoke a sense of connection and appreciation for the beauty that surrounds us. Thank you for joining me on this visual journey, and I hope my photography can bring you a glimpse of the joy and wonder I find in the world.

Cheers,
